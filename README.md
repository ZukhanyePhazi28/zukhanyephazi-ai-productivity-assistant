## Overview

The **AI Workplace Productivity Assistant** is a fully functional web app designed to help you streamline your work. With multiple AI-powered tools in one clean, responsive dashboard, you can:

- Automate routine tasks
- Summarize meetings
- Plan and prioritize work
- Generate professional emails
- Conduct AI-powered research
- Chat with a smart AI assistant

All outputs are editable, giving you full control over the final results. Every page includes a responsible AI disclaimer to encourage ethical use.

---

## Features ✨

### Dashboard
- Modern SaaS-style landing page
- Cards for each AI tool
- Collapsible sidebar
- Responsible AI disclaimer

### Smart Email Generator 📧
- Enter purpose, tone, recipient, and key points
- AI drafts a professional email
- Editable Markdown output

### Meeting Notes Summarizer 📝
- Paste raw notes
- Get structured summaries
- Key decisions, action items, follow-ups

### AI Task Planner ✅
- Describe a goal and timeline
- AI creates prioritized, actionable tasks
- Checkboxes for tracking progress

### AI Research Assistant 🔍
- Enter topic and depth level
- AI delivers structured research reports
- Suggestions for further reading

### AI Chatbot Interface 💬
- Real-time streaming chat
- Quick-action suggestions
- Markdown rendering and copy-to-clipboard
- Powered by Lovable AI Gateway
